Blood-Brain Barrier-Drug-Penetration-Predictions (BDPP)

To calculate the probability of a drug to penetrate the blood-brain barrier input physico-chemical descriptors or/and upload a png file of the skeletal formula of the compound. Click on "Calculation" to receive the probability scores.
